Publications & Bibliography

Known publications by G.Ya. Umarov and selected third-party references to his work

Over his career, Umarov authored 4 monographs, 6 popular science books, over 250 scientific articles, and received 31 patents. Below is a compilation of known publications drawn from primary source documents, citation records, and referenced bibliographies.

Monographs & Books

  • 1970
    "Beta-Spectrographs with Permanent Magnets"
    Co-authored with A.A. Abdurazzakov and G.M. Gromov
  • 1973
    "Biruni, Copernicus, and Modern Science"
    Translated to English as "At the Crossroads of the Millennium" (2001). Traces the heliocentric intellectual lineage from Al-Biruni to Copernicus.
  • 1974
    "Solar Energy"
    Co-authored with A.A. Yershov. Booklet on broad applications of solar energy.
  • 1976
    "Use of Low-Potential Solar Installations"
    Monograph on practical low-temperature solar thermal systems.
  • 1977
    "Theory and Calculation of Heliotechnical Concentrating Systems"
    Co-authored with R.A. Zakhidov and A.A. Vainer. Definitive treatment of solar concentrator mathematics.
